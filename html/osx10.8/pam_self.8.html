NAME
     pam_self -- Self PAM module

SYNOPSIS
     [service-name] function-class control-flag pam_self [options]

DESCRIPTION
     The Self PAM module supports the account management function class.  In
     terms of the function-class parameter, this is the ``account'' class.

     The Self account management module verifies that the username for the
     target account (PAM_USER) and the username of the applicant (PAM_RUSER)
     are the same.
