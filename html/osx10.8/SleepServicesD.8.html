NAME
     SleepServicesD -- Sleep Services daemon.

SYNOPSIS
DESCRIPTION
     Schedules dark wakes for the purpose of receiving push notifications, and
     caps dark wake time based on battery drain.

FILES
     /System/Library/CoreServices/SleepServicesD		   the daemon
     /System/Library/LaunchDaemons/com.apple.sleepservicesd.plist  launchd
								   configura-
								   tion plist
