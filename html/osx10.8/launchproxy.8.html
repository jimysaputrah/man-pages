NAME
     launchproxy -- inetd job emulation helper

SYNOPSIS
     launchproxy

DESCRIPTION
     launchproxy handles the inetd emulation for launchd.  This program may be
     merged into launchd in the future.

SEE ALSO
     launchctl(1), launchd.plist(5), launchd(8)
