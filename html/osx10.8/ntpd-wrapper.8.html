NAME
     ntpd-wrapper -- Wrapper for ntpdate/ntpd called by launchd

SYNOPSIS
     /usr/libexec/ntpd-wrapper

DESCRIPTION
     Invokes ntpdate followed by non-forking ntpd for launchd job org.ntp.ntpd

     Not intended for end users.
