NAME
     operator -- C operator precedence and order of evaluation

DESCRIPTION
	   Operator			   Associativity
	   --------			   -------------
	   () [] -> .			   left to right
	   ! ~ ++ -- - (type) * & sizeof   right to left
	   * / %			   left to right
	   + -				   left to right
	   << >>			   left to right
	   < <= > >=			   left to right
	   == !=			   left to right
	   &				   left to right
	   ^				   left to right
	   |				   left to right
	   &&				   left to right
	   ||				   left to right
	   ?:				   right to left
	   = += -= etc. 		   right to left
	   ,				   left to right

FILES
     /usr/share/misc/operator
