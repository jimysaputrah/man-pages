NAME
     corestoraged -- CoreStorage volume manager

SYNOPSIS
     corestoraged

DESCRIPTION
     corestoraged manages CoreStorage volumes.	It is invoked by launchd(8)
     when a CoreStorage volume needs management.

FILES
     /System/Library/LaunchDaemons/com.apple.corestorage.corestoraged.plist

SEE ALSO
     diskutil(8)
