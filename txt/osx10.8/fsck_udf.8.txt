NAME
     fsck_udf -- check a UDF filesystem

SYNOPSIS
     fsck_udf [-b blocksize] [-L] [-p] [-D] devicePath

DESCRIPTION
     The fsck_udf command examines a UDF filesystem residing on devicePath for
     correctness.

SEE ALSO
     mount_udf(8)

BUGS
     Currently does not perform any repairs.

HISTORY
     The fsck_udf utility first appeared in Mac OS X.
