NAME
       PPI::Token::Regexp::Substitute - A match and replace regular expression
       token

INHERITANCE
	 PPI::Token::Regexp::Substitute
	 isa PPI::Token::Regexp
	     isa PPI::Token
		 isa PPI::Element

SYNOPSIS
	 $text =~ s/find/$replace/;

DESCRIPTION
       A "PPI::Token::Regexp::Substitute" object represents a single
       substitution regular expression.

METHODS
       There are no methods available for "PPI::Token::Regexp::Substitute"
       beyond those provided by the parent PPI::Token::Regexp, PPI::Token and
       PPI::Element classes.

       Got any ideas for methods? Submit a report to rt.cpan.org!
