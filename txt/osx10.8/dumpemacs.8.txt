NAME
     dumpemacs -- Utility to dump pre-loaded emacs with compiled ELisp auto-
     loads

SYNOPSIS
     /usr/libexec/dumpemacs

DESCRIPTION
     Runs /usr/bin/emacs-undumped with standard ELisp auto-loads and dumps
     result in /usr/bin/emacs-$(arch)

SEE ALSO
     emacs(1,) emacs-undumped(1)
