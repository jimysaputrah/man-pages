NAME
     xml2man -- MPGL to mdoc (man page) translator

SYNOPSIS
     xml2man [-f] filename [output_filename]

ENVIRONMENT
     This program was designed to convert Man Page Generation Language (MPGL)
     XML files into mdoc-based manual pages.  The MPGL is a fairly direct
     translation of mdoc to XML.
