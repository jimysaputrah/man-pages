NAME
     odproxyd -- OpenDirectory proxy daemon

SYNOPSIS
     odproxyd [--version]

DESCRIPTION
     The odproxyd launchd(8) job maintains incoming proxy connections to the
     local opendirectoryd(8) daemon.  It should not be run manually.

SEE ALSO
     odutil(1), opendirectory(8)
