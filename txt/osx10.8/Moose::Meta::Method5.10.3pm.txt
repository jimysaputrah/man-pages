NAME
       Moose::Meta::Method - A Moose Method metaclass

DESCRIPTION
       This class is a subclass of Class::MOP::Method that provides additional
       Moose-specific functionality, all of which is private.

       To understand this class, you should read the the Class::MOP::Method
       documentation.

INHERITANCE
       "Moose::Meta::Method" is a subclass of Class::MOP::Method.
